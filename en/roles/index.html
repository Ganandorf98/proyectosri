    Roles and Objectives

    In BANG!, every player receives a secret role card that determines their goal in the game. Keep your identity hidden unless you are the Sheriff! [cite: 13]

    Role Descriptions
    Role Image Copies Objective Identity
    Sheriff 1 Eliminate all Outlaws and the Renegade. [cite: 13] Public
    Deputy 2 Protect the Sheriff at all costs. Share victory with them. [cite: 13] Hidden
    Outlaw 3 Kill the Sheriff. If the Sheriff dies, Outlaws win! [cite: 13] Hidden
    Renegade 1 Be the last man standing. Must kill the Sheriff in a final duel. [cite: 13] Hidden

    Role Distribution

    The number of roles in play depends on the total number of players: [cite: 13]

    • 4 players: 1 Sheriff, 1 Renegade, 2 Outlaws. [cite: 13]
    • 5 players: 1 Sheriff, 1 Renegade, 2 Outlaws, 1 Deputy. [cite: 13]
    • 6 players: 1 Sheriff, 1 Renegade, 3 Outlaws, 1 Deputy. [cite: 13]
    • 7 players: 1 Sheriff, 1 Renegade, 3 Outlaws, 2 Deputies. [cite: 13]

    Strategy

    As a Deputy, don't be afraid to take a hit for your Sheriff. As an Outlaw, coordination with other outlaws (even if you don't know for sure who they are) is key to victory.
