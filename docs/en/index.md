# Welcome to the Wild West! :material-cowboy:

This portal contains the fundamental rules for playing **BANG!**, the card game of gunfights, hidden roles, and outlaws.

### Quick Overview
* **Players:** 4 to 7 players.
* **Goal:** Depends on your secret role (Sheriff, Deputy, Outlaw, or Renegade).
* **Setup:** Each player receives a character with a unique ability and a specific number of bullets (life points).
